Our Story

We saw small businesses struggling with problems that had simple solutions. So we built them.

Core Bhive was founded in 2025 in Lahore with a straightforward mission — make professional HR, finance, and IT support accessible to every small and medium business in Pakistan.

For too long, quality outsourcing has been reserved for large corporations with big budgets. Small business owners were left juggling payroll, chasing invoices, fixing WiFi, and recruiting staff — all on top of actually running their business.

We built Core Bhive as a subscription-based outsourcing partner that works exactly like an in-house team, without the overhead. Our clients get dedicated professionals, monthly deliverables, and a single point of contact — at a fraction of what it would cost to hire internally.

Based in DHA Phase 6 Lahore, we work with businesses across the city and understand the specific challenges of operating in the Pakistani market.

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, reliable, and professional HR, financial, and IT outsourcing solutions that help small and medium businesses in Pakistan operate efficiently and grow sustainably.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's leading business outsourcing brand — where companies confidently delegate their operations knowing they have a trusted long-term partner behind them.
